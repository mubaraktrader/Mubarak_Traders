About Us

Mubarak Traders Corporation

Established in 1976, Mubarak Traders Corporation has built a legacy as a trusted name in the import, export, and distribution of high-quality spare parts for heavy machinery used in the Construction, Mining, Agriculture, and Roadbuilding sectors. With over four decades of industry experience, Mubarak Traders Corporation is managed and led by seasoned professionals committed to delivering excellence and reliability. Over the years, our company has evolved into a preferred partner for OEMs, dealers, and equipment repair facilities across Pakistan and international markets.

As a proud importer of globally recognized brands and a committed exporter to emerging markets, we also take pride in our very own brand, MTC (Mubarak Traders Corporation). MTC spare parts are manufactured to the highest standards to meet and exceed international quality benchmarks. These parts are engineered to perform reliably in even the most demanding work environments.

Mubarak Traders Corporation supplies a wide variety of spare parts for renowned brands such as Caterpillar, Cummins, Komatsu, JCB, Volvo, Hyundai, and many more. Whether it’s undercarriage components, engine parts, hydraulic systems, or ground engaging tools, our strong inventory and global supply chain ensure we meet the diverse needs of our customers.

At Mubarak Traders Corporation, quality is not just a standard — it’s a commitment. Every MTC product undergoes rigorous design, engineering, and quality control processes to ensure optimal performance. Our dedication to durability, precision, and cost-efficiency allows our clients to operate with complete confidence.

From concept to delivery, Mubarak Traders Corporation remains committed to its mission: To deliver dependable spare parts that keep machines working and businesses moving.

Why Choose Mubarak Traders Corporation?

With over four decades of experience, Mubarak Traders Corporation is a name synonymous with precision, performance, and trust. We offer an extensive range of high-quality spare parts for leading brands like Caterpillar, Komatsu, Cummins, Volvo, and more — engineered to withstand the toughest conditions. Our commitment to quality, global reach, and exceptional service makes us the preferred partner for OEMs, contractors, and repair facilities nationwide.
